Michael Jackson

1958 - 2009

The King of Pop

Michael Joseph Jackson is an American singer, songwriter, music producer, arranger, dancer, choreographer, actor, screenwriter, philanthropist, entrepreneur. The most successful performer in the history of pop music, known as the "King of Pop", winner of 15 Grammy awards and hundreds of other awards. Jackson's records (albums, singles, compilations, etc.) have sold over 1 billion copies worldwide. Michael Jackson has made significant contributions to popular music, music videos, dance and fashion.
